# Code of conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Critique ideas and implementations, not people.
- Explain statistical and engineering claims with evidence.
- Welcome questions from learners.
- Respect privacy and confidentiality.
- Accept responsibility, apologize, and repair harm when mistakes occur.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Deliberately misleading benchmarks or research claims.
- Sustained disruption of technical discussion.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer through the repository's contact channel. Maintainers may remove content, issue warnings, temporarily restrict participation, or permanently ban participants when necessary to protect the community.

This policy applies in project spaces and when representing the project publicly.
